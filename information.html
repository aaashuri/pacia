JUJUTSU KAISEN

Jujutsu Kaisen (呪術廻戦, "Sorcery Fight") is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated
by Gege Akutami. It has been serialized in Shueisha's shōnen manga magazine Weekly Shōnen Jump since March
2018, with its chapters collected and published in 24 tankōbon volumes as of October 2023.
